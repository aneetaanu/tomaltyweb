Boynton Beach Dental Insurance

The fees charged in our office are comparable to those charged by other area dentists. We are happy to provide assistance in submitting and interpreting your insurance coverage. For your convenience, insurance payments may be sent to this office by your insurance company. Most dental insurance is accepted at all of our locations. Our name may be found as dental providers for many of the major insurance companies. Major credit cards are accepted. Zero interest, extended patient payment plans are available by Carecredit and Citihealth financing.
